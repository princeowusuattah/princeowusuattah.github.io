HabitUp

A community-driven habit building app

Overview

  • HabitUp was initiated as a university visual design project. In this weekend project, I researched, conceptualized and built high fidelity visual design of an alarm app that could help people build new habits.

Role

  • User Research
  • Problem framing
  • Scoping
  • User flow
  • HiFi Visual Design

Duration

  • Weekend sprint (August 2017)

Tools

  • Illustrator
  • After effect
  • Overflow
  • Adobe XD

An alarm that helps users build new habits with their community's support.

Background

In our daily lives, we've attempted to keep up with time or activities with the help of alarms. As useful as alarms are they have limited effectiveness in helping users build around the activities it reminds them off. It practically doesn't help people to actively routinize their activities to become a habit. My goal is to create an alarm system that helps people build, maintain and psychologically automate new habits.
