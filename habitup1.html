Problem Space

Building a new habit is a subject that is often talked about but challenging to put into implementation. While most people desire to make an activity a habit, it is often met with all forms of inhibitors from themselves or their environment. Learning a new habit or changing from one can have a instantaneous impact on a person or be beneficial in their future.

Concept detail

Habit Up is a product concept that supports users to build new habit whiles fostering community interaction and support.

HabitUp acts as a digital repository of all the users' new habits and then reminds the users to complete the activity. Users can share their growth and follow their friend's activities as a source of motivation and support.

Scoping

My initial task was to gain insights into the overarching issues and challenges in habit-forming. This would help me align the focus of User research. Through journals articles, blog posts, magazines, social media posts, etc.

HabitUp was initiated as a university visual design project. In this project, I researched, conceptualized and built high fidelity visual design of an alarm app that could help people build new habits.
