What is Letsfund

Letsfund is a tool that facilitates peer to peer funding at any checkout. User can easily and seamlessly invite friends to help fund an item they desire to pay. The aims of Letsfund is to make shopping fun and more social.


To comply with my non-disclosure agreement, I have omitted and changed confidential information in this project. The designs presented here may differ slightly from the original, and do not necessarily reflect the views of client
