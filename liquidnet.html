Liquidnet Investment Analytics

Overview

From May to August 2018, I worked as an intern at Liquidnet's headquarters in New York for eleven weeks. Liquidnet is a worldwide institutional trading network that unites over 890 of the world's best asset managers in order to safely trade big blocks of assets without disrupting the market. Liquidnet's key business is its order management software program, which its clients use to trade Business to Business (B2B) and employs Artificial Intelligence (AI) to analyze market data for them. Fixed income, mutual funds, and all types of stocks are all supported by Liquidnet.
