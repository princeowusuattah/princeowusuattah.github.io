Redesigning the Miles Refer-a-friend

Overview

Refer a friend is one of Miles major feature within it’s growth strategy. The aim of this project was to solve attributtion issues and unify the user experience of refering friends and build brand trust through messaging

Deliverables

  • User Interviews
  • User personas
  • Information architecture
  • User-flow
  • HiFi Visual Design

  • Advised by

Context

Problem Space

Building a new habit is a subject that is often talked about but challenging to put into implementation. While most people desire to make an activity a habit, it is often met with all forms of inhibitors from themselves or their environment. Learning a new habit or changing from one can have a instantaneous impact on a person or be beneficial in their future.
